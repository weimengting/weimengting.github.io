Mengting Wei is now a PhD student in University of Oulu where she works in the Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS) under the supervision of Prof. Guoying Zhao. She is also a PhD student in European Laboratory for Learning and Intelligent Systems (ELLIS), co-supervised by Prof. Nuria Oliver. Previously, she graduated from Southeast University with a master degree, supervised by Prof. Wenming Zheng. She received her bechelor degree from Hebei University of Technology. Her current research interests lie at Facial Expression Recognition.
