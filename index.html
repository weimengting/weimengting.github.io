Mengting Wei (魏梦婷)

Master Degree Candidate
Mengting Wei is a postgraduate student in Southeast University where she works in the Affective Information Processing Lab. Previously, she graduated from Hebei University of technology with a bechelor degree. Her current research interests lie at Facial Micro-Expression Recognition.
